3. MAIN BUSINESS RULE

The balance must always be calculated automatically.

Use:

Total Charges = Sum of all charges added for the student

Total Paid = Sum of all payments made by the student

Remaining Balance = Total Charges - Total Paid

Example:

Monthly Fee = ₹1,000
Books = ₹2,500
Dress = ₹1,200

Total Charges = ₹4,700

Payments:
₹2,000
₹1,000

Total Paid = ₹3,000

Remaining Balance = ₹1,700

Never allow the user to manually enter or edit the remaining balance.
